# Privacy Policy – Nature's Infusions / Ultra-Simple  Static EStore

Effective Date: 2025-07-01

## 1. No Tracking, No Cookies, No Analytics
This website does not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies. We do not collect, store, or share your browsing data.

## 2. Information We Collect
We only collect information that you voluntarily provide when:
- Placing an order (name, shipping address, PayPal email for payment)
- Submitting a support request (name, email, and message)
- Leaving a product review (name and review content, optional)

All payments are processed securely via PayPal. We do not store or process your payment details on this website.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
Your information is used solely to:
- Process and fulfill your order
- Respond to your support requests
- Display your product reviews (if submitted)

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties, except as required to process your order (e.g., with PayPal for payment).

## 4. Data Security
All data is stored securely and is only accessible to the solo developer responsible for site maintenance and support.

## 5. Data Retention
Order and support information is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill your request and comply with legal obligations.

## 6. Your Rights
You may request to view or delete your personal information at any time by contacting support@naturesinfusions.com.

## 7. Contact
For privacy questions or requests, contact:
support@naturesinfusions.com

---

This privacy policy is consistent with the Ultra-Simple  Static EStore Framework's commitment to privacy, minimal data collection, and small business best practices.
