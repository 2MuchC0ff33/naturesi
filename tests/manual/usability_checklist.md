# Usability Manual Checklist

Key flows:

- Search: perform a search and verify meaningful results and accessible focus order
- Add to cart: ensure the action is discoverable and provides feedback
- Cart: ensure quantities are editable and errors are surfaced
- Checkout: ensure explanatory text, no surprises, and clear pay button

Checks:

- [ ] Buttons have descriptive text
- [ ] Error states are announced (role="alert") and useful
- [ ] Form validation messages are clear and keyboard accessible
- [ ] Links to policies (privacy, terms) are present and visible
